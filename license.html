§ 1. DEFINITIONS

For purposes of this Agreement, the following definitions apply:

  • "Nexus Content" means any and all files, scripts, assets, tools, guides, datamined data, rescripted clients, leaked materials, SVG assets, images, documentation, source code, or any other material made available through Nexus Collective's platforms, Discord channels, or direct member sharing.
  • "Authentication Data" means your Discord account identity, Discord user ID, Discord username, Discord avatar, associated email address, and any OAuth tokens used during the login process.
  • "Session Data" means your IP address, timezone, browser information, login timestamps, session duration, and frequency of access recorded at the time of and during your use of the Platform.
  • "Identifying Data" collectively refers to Authentication Data and Session Data.
  • "Platform" means this website, any Nexus-associated Discord server, shared file repositories, or any other system under Nexus Collective's control or operation.
  • "Administrator" means any individual designated by Nexus Collective with elevated permissions, including Super Administrators, who have access to the administrative dashboard and stored member data.
  • "Leak" or "Unauthorized Distribution" means the sharing, publication, sale, upload, transmission, or any other form of distribution of Nexus Content to individuals or entities outside of the Nexus Collective Platform without prior written authorization from an Administrator.

§ 2. IP ADDRESS COLLECTION, STORAGE, AND USE

This section is critical. Read it carefully before proceeding.

By authenticating with this Platform, you expressly and irrevocably consent to the collection, storage, and use of your Internet Protocol (IP) address and associated session metadata. This consent is a mandatory condition of membership and cannot be waived or withdrawn without terminating your membership entirely.

2.1 What We Collect

  • Your public IP address as detected at the time of each login event.
  • Your timezone as reported by your browser at the time of authentication.
  • The date and time of every login event, accurate to the second.
  • A monthly key associating each login with a calendar month for pattern analysis.
  • Your Discord User ID, username, and avatar as provided by Discord's OAuth system.
  • Your associated email address as held in the authentication system.

2.2 How We Use This Data

  • Security & Fraud Prevention: IP logs are analyzed to detect account sharing, unauthorized access, credential theft, and suspicious geographic anomalies. Accounts that log in from three (3) or more distinct IP addresses within a single calendar month are automatically flagged and suspended pending Administrator review.
  • Leak Investigation & Attribution: In the event that Nexus Content is found to have been leaked, distributed, or published without authorization, stored IP addresses and Identifying Data may be used to identify the responsible member(s) and to establish identity for the purpose of pursuing legal remedies.
  • Legal Proceedings: Nexus Collective reserves the right to provide stored Identifying Data — including but not limited to IP addresses, Discord IDs, email addresses, and session logs — to law enforcement agencies, attorneys, arbitrators, or courts of competent jurisdiction in connection with any investigation, civil claim, or criminal proceeding arising from a violation of this Agreement.
  • Access Control: IP data is used to enforce account lockouts, rate limits, and geographic restrictions as determined by Platform Administrators.
  • Administrative Oversight: Administrators have access to a dashboard displaying IP logs, session records, and login history for all members. This data is used to maintain the integrity, safety, and exclusivity of the Platform.

2.3 Data Retention

IP address logs and session records are retained indefinitely, or until Nexus Collective chooses to purge them at Administrator discretion. Member account deletion does not automatically result in the deletion of historical IP logs. In cases where a legal hold is active — meaning data is being preserved for ongoing or anticipated legal action — no data will be deleted until that hold is lifted.

2.4 No Anonymity Guarantee

You acknowledge and accept that use of this Platform does not guarantee anonymity. If you violate this Agreement, your Identifying Data will be used against you. The use of VPNs, proxies, Tor exit nodes, or similar anonymization tools does not eliminate your legal exposure and may itself be treated as suspicious activity resulting in account suspension.

§ 3. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY & ANTI-LEAK POLICY

3.1 Nexus Asset Protection

All files, scripts, assets, and tools distributed through this Platform are protected under Nexus Asset Protection (NAP). NAP applies automatically to every piece of content shared within the Platform, regardless of whether it is individually watermarked, labeled, or otherwise marked as protected.

3.2 Prohibited Distribution

You are strictly prohibited from:

  • Sharing, publishing, uploading, selling, or otherwise distributing any Nexus Content to individuals outside the Platform.
  • Reproducing Nexus Content on any public or private server, forum, Discord, website, social media platform, or file-sharing service without written Administrator authorization.
  • Reselling or commercializing Nexus Content under your own brand or through a third party.
  • Providing access to the Platform — directly or indirectly — to non-members.
  • Circumventing access controls, authentication mechanisms, or IP-logging systems.

3.3 Consequences of Leaking

Any member found to have leaked or unauthorized distributed Nexus Content will face the following consequences, applied at Administrator discretion based on severity:

  • Immediate, permanent account ban from all Nexus platforms and associated Discord servers.
  • Disclosure of Identifying Data (IP address, Discord ID, email, session logs) to relevant parties for the purpose of civil or criminal legal action.
  • Civil claim for damages arising from the unauthorized distribution, including but not limited to reputational harm, loss of competitive advantage, and costs incurred in identifying and prosecuting the leak.
  • Reporting to Discord Trust & Safety and any other relevant platform with documented evidence of the violation.

You acknowledge that Nexus Collective has the technical means — through stored IP addresses, Discord identities, and session records — to identify you in the event of a leak. You are explicitly warned that leaking is not anonymous, and will be pursued.
