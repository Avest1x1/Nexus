Leaking, Unauthorized Sharing & Legal Consequences

This section carries the most serious consequences of any clause in this agreement. Read it carefully.

If you leak, share, redistribute, sell, or in any way cause the unauthorized circulation of any Asset obtained through this platform — regardless of whether it was your direct intent, whether it was a "small" file, or whether you believe the asset to be freely available elsewhere — the following will occur:

05

Membership Tiers, Access Control & Approval Process

All accounts begin in a Pending state upon first login. Pending accounts are registered and logged, but have no access to any assets, files, or gated content. This is not a locked state — you can use the platform freely — but resource access is not granted until an admin manually assigns your membership tier.

Membership tiers and their access levels are as follows:

  • Pending — Registered, awaiting approval. No resource access.
  • Member — Approved. Access to community assets and general utility resources.
  • Trusted — Vetted member. Broader access including community drops and system modules.
  • Highly Trusted — Engine-level and advanced resources. Official files included.
  • Mommy's Favorite — Full vault access including private and unreleased assets. No request path exists for this tier.

Tier assignments are made entirely at administrator discretion. There is no formal request process. Demonstrating value to the community, contributing assets, and being a reliable member are the only paths to advancement.

06

Account Security, IP Verification & Lockouts

Your account is tied to the IP address recorded at registration. This is version 1 of your identity record ("original_ip") and it does not change. On every login, your current IP is compared against this record.

If a discrepancy is detected, the following occurs automatically and without prior warning:

  • Your account is immediately locked
  • You are redirected to a locked state page and cannot access any other part of the platform
  • The new IP is logged as evidence
  • An admin must manually review and unlock your account

To appeal a lockout, join the Lymeware Discord server and open an admin ticket. Be prepared to verify your identity. Lockouts caused by VPN use, network changes, or travel are handled case-by-case. There are no automatic unlocks.

You are responsible for your account security. Do not share your Discord login with anyone. Account compromise does not exempt you from the consequences of any actions taken under your account.

Administrators operate under a separate internal conduct policy. Abuse of administrator privileges, including unauthorized data access, is grounds for immediate removal and potential legal action.
