Nature of the Platform & Scope of Agreement

Nexus Collective is a private, invite-only resource-sharing platform exclusively accessible to vetted members of the Lymeware Industries community. The platform distributes game modification assets, decompiled scripts, system modules, and related development resources among registered members.

This Agreement governs all access to, use of, and conduct within the platform. It applies retroactively to all content you have received, downloaded, or used from this platform regardless of when access was granted. Membership is a privilege, not a right, and may be revoked at any time at administrator discretion.

Entry to Nexus Collective is exclusively via Lymeware Industries. Attempting to access the platform through other means, sharing invitation links, or circumventing the application process constitutes an immediate and permanent ban with no right of appeal.

02

Data Collection, IP Logging & Consent

By proceeding with Discord authentication, you expressly and irrevocably consent to the collection, storage, and processing of the following personal data:

  • Your Discord username, user ID, and associated email address
  • Your public IP address, recorded at the time of first registration
  • Your IP address on every subsequent login session
  • Your device timezone and approximate geographic region
  • Your phone number if provided to and shared by Discord's OAuth API
  • Timestamps of all login events, session durations, and access logs

IP address data is used for security verification and identity continuity. If your IP changes from the value recorded at registration, your account will be automatically suspended pending administrator review. This is non-negotiable. There are no exceptions.

This data is never sold, shared with third parties, or used for advertising. It is retained for security, dispute resolution, and legal compliance for as long as your account exists, and for a minimum of 24 months following account termination.

Note: Discord's OAuth2 API does not expose phone numbers by default regardless of scope. If your number was not recorded this is expected behavior from Discord, not a platform error.

Leaking, Unauthorized Sharing & Legal Consequences

This section carries the most serious consequences of any clause in this agreement. Read it carefully.

If you leak, share, redistribute, sell, or in any way cause the unauthorized circulation of any Asset obtained through this platform — regardless of intent, file size, or whether you believe the asset is freely available elsewhere — the following will occur:

  • You will be contacted directly. We will locate your Discord account and a Nexus admin will personally reach out with documented evidence of the leak.

This agreement survives account termination indefinitely. The above applies even if you are no longer a member at the time of discovery.

05

Membership Tiers, Access Control & Approval Process

All accounts begin in a Pending state upon first login. Pending accounts are registered and logged, but have no access to any assets or gated content. Access is not granted until an admin manually assigns your membership tier.

  • Pending — Registered, awaiting approval. No resource access.
  • Member — Approved. Community assets and general utility resources.
  • Trusted — Vetted member. Broader access including community drops and system modules.
  • Highly Trusted — Engine-level and advanced resources. Official files included.
  • Mommy's Favorite — Full vault access including private and unreleased assets. No request path.

Tier assignments are made entirely at administrator discretion. There is no formal request process. Contributing to the community is the only reliable path to advancement.

06

Account Security, IP Verification & Lockouts

Your account is tied to the IP address recorded at registration. On every login, your current IP is compared against this record. If a discrepancy is detected the following occurs automatically and without prior warning:

  • Your account is immediately locked
  • You are shown a locked state and cannot access any other part of the platform
  • The new IP is logged as evidence
  • An admin must manually review and unlock your account

To appeal, join the Lymeware Discord and open an admin ticket. Lockouts caused by VPN use, network changes, or travel are handled case-by-case. There are no automatic unlocks.

07

Administrator Rights & Oversight

Administrators hold elevated privileges for platform security and member management. Administrator actions include:

  • Viewing member profiles including IP address records for security verification
  • Approving, modifying, or revoking membership tier assignments
  • Locking or permanently banning accounts
  • Accessing session and login logs for any account

Administrator accounts are subject to reduced data collection: IP addresses are not logged for admin accounts. Timezone data is still recorded for session management.
